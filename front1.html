About Air Quality

Air quality refers to the condition of the air within our surroundings. It is determined by the amount of pollutants present, which can affect human health, the environment, and even the economy. Poor air quality can lead to respiratory diseases, heart conditions, and can aggravate existing health problems.

Why Air Quality Check is Needed

Monitoring air quality is crucial for several reasons:

  • Health Protection: Regularly checking air quality helps in protecting public health by identifying and reducing exposure to harmful pollutants.
  • Environmental Impact: Understanding air quality helps in mitigating the effects of pollution on the environment, including plants, animals, and water bodies.
  • Regulatory Compliance: It aids governments and organizations in ensuring compliance with air quality standards and regulations.
  • Public Awareness: It raises awareness among the public about the quality of air they breathe and encourages them to take actions to improve it.
